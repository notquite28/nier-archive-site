# Inspiration: Ending E

## The Original Vision

In Nier Automata's Ending E, something remarkable happens. After defeating the credits (yes, the credits are a boss), players are asked a question: will you sacrifice your save data to help another player who is struggling?

Those who say yes lose everything. Their saves, erased. But in exchange, their data becomes part of a collective - appearing as support units for other players fighting the same battle.

## Shared Presence

This website is a humble homage to that idea. Instead of save data, you share your presence. The cursors you see are real people, exploring this space at the same time as you.

There's no goal here. No objective to complete. Just the quiet knowledge that others are here too.

## The Pods' Words

> "Everything that lives is designed to end. We are perpetually trapped in a never-ending spiral of life and death."

But perhaps connection - however fleeting - gives meaning to that spiral.

## Technical Poetry

The implementation is simple on purpose. A WebSocket server. Cursor positions. Real-time updates. But the effect is something more than the sum of its parts.

Sometimes the simplest technology creates the most profound experiences.
